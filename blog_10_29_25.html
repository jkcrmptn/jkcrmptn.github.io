New Mod Release

KF-SpookyManor for Killing Floor

Steam WorkshopModDB

General Information

The Halloween season is upon you, this manor which was once decaying and dark, is now decaying and lit up! Lights cover the outside of the house, but a strange stench of death is around the whole area, and was that water always red? Anyways, you are your team better think of where you are going to hold out, and fast.

This is a Halloween re-imagining of KF-Manor called KF-SpookyManor, This was started on the first week of October this year. The layout is the same, and gameplay is mostly the same, only difference is the caves were lightly nerfed, and the zeds now spawn much faster.


Please report any bugs you find.

This map features custom music, likely not copyright safe.

I highly recommend using direct download I had to separate it into 2 parts due to workshop size limitations.
